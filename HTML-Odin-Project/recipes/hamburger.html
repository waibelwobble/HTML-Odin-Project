Hamburger


Delicious burgers for the entire family to enjoy at the next family barbecue!
    Ingredients!
  • Beef patties
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Bacon
    Instructions!
  1. Start off by cooking the bacon.
  2. Grill the patties.
  3. Put the patties on a bun.
  4. Put the tomato, cooked bacon, and lettuce on the patty.
  5. Enjoy!
