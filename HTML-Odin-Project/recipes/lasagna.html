Lasagna


Delicious vegetarian lasagna for the whole family to enjoy! Mushrooms and spinach fill in for the usual beef in this amazing recipe!
    Ingredients!
  • Mushrooms
  • Spinach
  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Marinara
    Instructions!
  1. Put pasta down.
  2. Put cheese on pasta.
  3. Put spinach and mushrooms on the cheese.
  4. Put the marinara over the whole thing.
  5. Bake it! Somehow, with an oven, maybe.
